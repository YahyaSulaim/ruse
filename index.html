Murder Mystery: "The Last Night"

On a crisp autumn evening, Clara Thompson, the much-loved owner of Clara’s Corner Café, hosted a cozy gathering for her closest friends. The café, adorned with fairy lights and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee, was a charming setting for the occasion. Clara wanted to celebrate her recent engagement to Jack Dawson, a local mechanic she had grown to love deeply.

However, beneath the warmth of the evening, tensions simmered. Clara’s best friend, Lily Carter, had secretly been in love with Jack for years, making the engagement news painful for her. Clara’s estranged sister, Emma Grant, also harbored resentment, bitter that Clara inherited their family home, a sore spot between them.

As the night went on, everyone laughed and mingled, blissfully unaware that it would be their last night together. By morning, Clara was dead.
